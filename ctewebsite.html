Technology Student Association(TSA)

About TSA

TSA is a new CTE program to Manila High School as of the 2018-2019 school year advised by Christina Barrentine and Gerri McCann. TSA encourages students of Manila High School to develop their skills in STEM related-fields through competitive events, the robotics program, and engineering curriculum acquired through Project Lead The Way.

Future Business Leaders of America(FBLA)

About FBLA

FBLA is a CTE program at Manila High School advised by Tonia Eubanks that prepares Manila High School students for their professional lives through teaching proper business procedure and competitive events. Manila High School also offers business classes to prepare you for life after high school.
